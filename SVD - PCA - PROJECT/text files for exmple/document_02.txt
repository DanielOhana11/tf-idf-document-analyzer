Birthday Cake Recipe: Celebrating in Style

A birthday celebration isn't complete without a delicious homemade cake. This classic vanilla birthday cake recipe is perfect for any age and can be customized with your favorite flavors and decorations.

To make the cake, you'll need 2½ cups of all-purpose flour, 2½ teaspoons of baking powder, ½ teaspoon of salt, 1 cup of softened butter, 2 cups of sugar, 4 large eggs, 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract, and 1¼ cups of whole milk. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.

Start by creaming the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, which takes about 3-4 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, then stir in the vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.

Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let the cakes cool completely before frosting.

For the frosting, beat together butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and a splash of milk until smooth and creamy. Frost the cooled cake layers and decorate with sprinkles, candles, or fresh fruit for a festive touch.
